Empowers editors

  • Allow whomever you'd like to submit to your publication, including everyone
  • Schedule staff meetings, where all your staff can review and score submissions
  • Review highest-scored submissions and publish the best with one click
  • Design a unique aesthetic and flow for each publication you publish
  • Manage both submissions intended for print and purely digital media

Organizes organizers

  • Choose from customizable, beautiful, and responsive templates for your blog
  • Schedule blog posts (either from published/upcoming content, or custom posts)
  • Organize a store and customize what person or group gets shipment requests
  • Track your publication's health with beautiful charts: meeting attendance, awarded scores for current and historic publications, number of submissions issue-over-issue, and more

Makes makers happy

  • Have complete control over how your submissions will look, feel, and/or sound, once published on the web
  • Check on the status of your submitted works, such as if they've been reviewed yet
  • Easily submit under a pseudonym, if desired
  • If the editor enables it, see how your submissions are scored even before they're published
  • Accumulate an impressive public profile as you're published in various issues and publications that use the Publishist platform
  • Maintain a complete history of your submitted works on your private profile, and easily revise and resubmit unaccepted works to the same or other publications
  • Monetize your creations by adding Flattr, PayPal, Dwolla, Bitcoin, and other donation options to the footer of your works, or just your profile page
  • Upload audio versions of your written works, if you'd like, or allow your fans to contribute audio versions!
  • Have complete control of the licensing of each of your works, whether strict copyright or joyful copyleft
  • Publicize upcoming performances to fans so they can come support you in person

Publishist aims to promote open communities of people who joyfully create more than they consume. We hope to be a mechanism by which creators are lovingly critiqued and championed by their community (because no one grows in a vacuum), and by which the best works are given appropriate promotion above all the noise. We believe in the ability of localized communities to produce artists who produce creations on par with the best creations of history.
